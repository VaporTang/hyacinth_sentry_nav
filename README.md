## 0. Hyacinth
When hyacinths bloom, they bear a beauty that is nothing short of soul-stirring. That shade of purple mirrors the melancholy yet profound love I hold in my heart, while concealing a guilt and remorse I find impossible to voice.
​I am fully aware that I can be eccentric—perhaps even difficult to deal with—and that getting close to me requires courage. Yet, I remain absolutely certain of one thing: if you are willing to accept this singular love, I will grant you a happiness that no one else could ever hope to match.
